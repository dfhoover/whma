White Crane - Matsumura Hakutsura

Overview

Matsumra Hakutsura White Crane is a graceful, upright fighting system focusing on pressure points and nerve strikes instead of blunt force punches and kicks. It was developed by Matsumura Sokon, a legendary pioneer of Okinawan Karate. He founded Shorin-ryu Karate and taught Gichin Funakoshi who went on to establish Shotokan Karate. Matsumura developed this unique White Crane style later in his life, and is a Karateâ€™ified system of Chinese White Crane.

Sung He

Keys
  • Sprockets: large sprocket moves small sprocket. From wrist grab, I rotate my wrist, then turn body & catch under his forearm

Keys
