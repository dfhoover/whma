Karate • Requirements to Grade

Shodan to Nidan

Nidan candidates must meet all of the requirements of Shodan, along with the following additions:

Nyumon
  1. Kake (1..17)
  2. Tegumi (1..10 + Heishu & Kaishu waza futari geiko)
  3. Tsuki waza futari geiko*
  4. Shiho Uke waza / Bear hug drill
  5. Taisabaki Oyo-waza / Exits (1,2,4,5,6)
  6. Shiho Uke waza / Quadrant Drill (optional)
  7. Kansetsu waza / Pressure Points (optional)
Kata
  1. Shisochin
  2. Seisan
  3. Sanchin Dai
  4. Tensho Dai
  5. Futari no Kata
Kobudo Kata
1. Kyu no Kon*
2. One of the following:
  1. Chatanyara no sai || Matsu higa no sai
  2. Tachi Seiho 1..6, Niten Ichi Ryu || Seitei Kata 1..6
  3. Nunchaku kihon kata
Musha shugy≈ç
A foreign kata or approved equivalent.

Written Requirements
  1. Written test (including 36 Habitual Acts of Physical Violence)
  2. Essay: The History of Karate
  3. Martial Arts Logbook
  4. Two book reports
  5. Resume
Time
Minimum of two years of training at shodan, with minimum of six months from time of submission of Request to Grade. Class time and assisting time.
