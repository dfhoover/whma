White Crane

A graceful, upright fighting system focusing on pressure points and nerve strikes instead of forceful punches and kicks, this is Matsumura Hakutsuru, the White Crane school founded by Matsumura Sokon.

Video

Matsumura Sokon is a legendary pioneer of Okinawan Karate. He founded Shorin-ryu Karate and mentored Gichin Funakoshi, who went on to develop Shotokan Karate. Matsumura developed this unique White Crane style later in his life. Matsumura’s White Crane is a Karate’ified system of Chinese White Crane. As mentioned above, in place of the traditional punches and high kicks one finds in Karate, White Crane instead focuses on pressure points and striking nerves.

Due to the severe nature of the techniques Hanshi Legacy only teaches this system to nidans and above. In fact, it has only been recently that Hanshi has started teaching this system outside of own school. I am humbled and privileged to be studying the Crane at Renshi Fries' Heritage Martial Arts.
