White Crane

Hanshi Legacy and a handful of his senior students demonstrated at Capital Conquest, a Canadian martial arts mecca, back in November. Check out this video of his demonstration while it is posted.

Video

This is a demonstration of some of the warm-up drills of Matsumura Hakutsura White Crane. This is the White Crane of Karate pioneer Matsumura Sokon. The school is a Karate'ified system of Chinese White Crane.

Due to the severe nature of the techniques Hanshi Legacy only teaches this system to nidans and above. In fact, it has only been recently that Hanshi has started teaching this system outside of own school. I am priviledged to be studying the Crane at Renshi Fries' Heritage Martial Arts.
