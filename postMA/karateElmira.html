Karate Elmira

A shout out to Renshi Ryan Potter! And a reminder to folks that Karate continues in Elmira at the Woolwich Memorial Centre! Renshi Ryan Potter and Driftwood Martial Arts have picked up the torch and are continuing teaching youth and adult Karate classes at the WMC. I’ve spent many gradings and seminars with Renshi Potter and I even had the privilege of sitting on his yondan grading panel. Renshi Potter knows Karate, moves like a cat, and radiates energy and enthusiasm. If you’ve been thinking of trying Karate or your child has been asking, I strongly recommend you check out his classes. For more info: https://driftwoodkarate.com/program/elmira-woolwich-karate/
